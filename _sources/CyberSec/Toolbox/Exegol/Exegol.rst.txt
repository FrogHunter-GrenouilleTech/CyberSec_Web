Exegol is a community-driven hacking environment, powerful and yet simple enough to be used by
anyone in day to day engagements. Exegol is the best solution to deploy powerful hacking
environments securely, easily, professionally. Exegol fits pentesters, CTF players, bug bounty
hunters, researchers, beginners and advanced users, defenders, from stylish macOS users and
corporate Windows pros to UNIX-like power users.
